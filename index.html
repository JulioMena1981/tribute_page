Albert Einstein

1879 - 1955

The most important, well-known and popular scientist of the 20th century

Albert Einstein (German: / ˈalbɛɐ̯t ˈʔaɪnʃtaɪn /; Ulm, German Empire; March 14, 1879 - Princeton, United States; April 18, 1955) was a German physicist of Jewish origin, later nationalized Swiss, Austrian and American. He is considered the most important, well-known and popular scientist of the 20th century.
